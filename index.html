About Me

Info

Hello! Hope all is well. My name is Kelenna Caleb Azu, but most people simply call me Caleb so please feel free to do the same. I’m an aspiring model with a keen eye for detail and a deep love for storytelling through visual media.

Since I was a kid, I’ve been captivated by photography how a single image can speak a thousand words. I loved immersing myself in moments and finding ways to preserve them through the lens. A clean shot doesn’t just freeze time; it reveals one of the many faces we carry.

In my heart, modeling is more than posing it’s about becoming a vessel for emotion and connection, inviting curiosity, and leaving behind something that lingers.
